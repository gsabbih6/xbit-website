## Design Context

### Users
Founders, technical leaders, and business owners looking for a capable engineering partner to build production-grade software. They value competence, speed, and clarity. They want to know "Can this team build what I need?" and "Are they professional?"

### Brand Personality
Confident, competent, highly technical but accessible, straightforward, and "no bullshit." (We are "Consultants Who Ship.")

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone**: Brutally minimal and highly focused. Dark mode by default (`bg-brand-dark`), accented by a high-contrast teal (`brand-primary`). 
- **Typography**: Bold, confident display fonts for headings; clean and legible body fonts. Fluid clamp sizing for responsiveness.
- **Constraints**: No gradient text, no generic "AI slop" glassmorphism, no unnecessary cards-within-cards. Everything must earn its place.
- **Layout**: Asymmetric grids, distinct visual rhythm, and flat surfaces (removed ambient radial glows during distillation).

### Design Principles
1. **Show, Don't Tell**: Let the shipped products (XRPay, MolSim, Meshada) speak for the capability.
2. **Ruthless Simplification**: Remove anything that isn't absolutely necessary. Cut marketing fluff; use concise, active voice.
3. **High Signal-to-Noise**: Provide clear, specific actions (e.g., specific form labels like "Work email" instead of "Email").
4. **Purposeful Interaction**: Animations and interactive states should serve a clear purpose (e.g., guiding focus) rather than just being decorative.
